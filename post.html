We are suggesting some solutions for your subject selection with ICT

These solutions will guide you...

Posted by ICT Foundation according to new syllabus 2019 onwards.

A/L ICT Subject Selection

A/L ICT provides a very high Z-Score irrespective of the stream. The subject content is suitable for students doing A/Ls in any stream. A/L ICT provides a solid foundation for the students who wants to proceed in the field of Computer Science.

The subject content almost reaches the content of an undergraduate programme.

A/L Commerce Stream with ICT
You have to select at least two commerce subjects to fall under this stream. The following selection is recommended in commerce stream based on the undergraduate programmes allowed in the universities.

Highly recommended = Accounting + ICT + Economics
Next recommended option = Accounting + ICT + Business Studies

Please note that other than the above many other selections are possible. But above are the most recommended.

A/L Other Stream with ICT
You have to select at least two science subjects to fall under this stream. The following selection is recommended in other stream based on the undergraduate programmes allowed in the universities.

Highly recommended = Combined Maths + ICT + Physics
Next recommended option = Biology + ICT + Physics

Please note that other than the above many other selections are possible. But above are the most recommended.

A/L Technology Stream with ICT
You have to select at least two technology subjects to fall under this stream. The following selection is recommended in technology stream based on the undergraduate programmes allowed in the universities.

Highly recommended = Engineering Tech. + ICT + Science for Tech.

A/L Arts Stream with ICT
You have to select at least two Arts subjects to fall under this stream. The following selection is recommended in Arts stream based on the undergraduate programmes allowed in the universities.

Highly recommended = Two Languages or Literature + ICT

In this case the most difficult subject for you will be the ICT subject. But based on the above combination you'll be able to focus more to the ICT subject. And you'll definitely end up in a good rank.
